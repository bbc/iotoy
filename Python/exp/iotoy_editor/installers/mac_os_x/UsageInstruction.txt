How to use this graphical loader
================================

1. Switch off your device (if appropriate to next step)

2. Switch your device on, in DFU update mode

3. Confirm that your device is in DFU update mode
   (maybe you have lights that come on)

4. Hit the "program device" button:
   - A file selector will open
   - Find your hex file
   - Open it
   - Your device will now be being programmed

5. Wait until programming is complete

6. Unplug/restart your device
